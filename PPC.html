About Us

Welcome to our Korean Dessert Food Truck! We specialize in serving delicious Korean desserts made with high-quality ingredients. Our menu includes a variety of popular treats, including Taiyaki Ice Cream with fish-shaped waffle cones, soft-serve icecream, colorful mochi, and Rilakkuma ice cream. Come and eat these special treats on the street!








Our Menu

Taiyaki Ice Cream

A delightful treat consisting of a fish-shaped waffle cone filled with creamy ice cream flavors of your choice.

Mochi Donuts

Soft and chewy mochi in various flavors, including matcha, red bean, and strawberry.

Rilakkuma Ice Cream

Cute and delicious ice cream cones topped with adorable Rilakkuma characters made of chocolate and cookies.
